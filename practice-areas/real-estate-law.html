Real Estate Law

Experienced Attorneys for Life’s Biggest Investment

Buying a first home? Leasing a commercial property? Developing a subdivision?

Buying and selling real estate, for most people, represents among the largest financial transactions of their lives. It also is an area where people can find themselves quickly in trouble. Yet, property buyers will sometimes risk it without an attorney or go for the lowest-price option.

Whether you are a developer, landlord or a first-time homeowner, there is too much on the line to risk running into avoidable legal complications. The experienced attorneys at Ghana Royalty have handled hundreds of real estate transactions and disputes. We know the pitfalls that await both buyers and sellers, whether buying a home, condominium or commercial property.

Real estate transactions are tricky business for the inexperienced. Protect yourself and your most important investments when it comes to:
  • Purchases and sales
  • Landlord-tenant litigation
  • Residential and commercial leases
  • Zoning and land use
  • Land and lot-line disputes
  • Subdivision
  • Condominium
  • Real estate litigation
  • Professional land acquisition and development
  • Permitting for business and residential uses
