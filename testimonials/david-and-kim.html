Testimonials > David and Kim

NAME: David and Kim
LOCATION: Wakefield
OCCUPATION: Former Biomedical Engineer and Administrative Assistant
LEGAL SERVICES: Personal Injury and Estate Planning

What sets Ghana Royalty apart?

David’s personal injury case spanned many years, and we had no idea how long it would go on or how hard it would be when we started. It was a very difficult, emotional case, and Ghana Royalty took the time to sit and explain everything to us and give us the strength to go on when we didn’t think we could.

David Lucas is not only fantastic at what he does, but he’s also a kind, compassionate man that cares deeply about his clients. Because of this, we now consider him not only our lawyer, but also our friend.

What do you look for in a firm?

What was most important to us was finding someone who truly cared about what we were going through and would help us find the direction we needed. We were overwhelmed with not only the medical issues, but also the legalities involved. David Lucas took the time to explain everything and truly cared about what we were going through. It was a very complex case, and he did everything he could to make sure we got the best possible outcome.

Would you recommend Ghana Royalty to others?

Without hesitation. We have never had a lawyer who treated us so well or that has been so genuine, honest, and caring about every detail of our situation.
